EECS ABET Tools

At the Beginning of the Term

At the beginning of the term you will be required to provide assessment information for each CLO targeted by your course. Underneath the course information listed at the top of the page, you will see a list of CLOs. If there are any curriculum improvement messages for a program outcome assessed by a CLO, they will be listed below that CLO. Below that, you will find assessment information from the last term that this course was taught.

Fill in the assessment method and satisfactory scores for each CLO. If a CLO is assessed in more than one area of the course, click the button below the table to add another row. Finally, you will be required to fill in the comment box below all of the CLOs. If you have no input, just say "None." Once finished, click the 'Approve Button'.

After Approval

After initially approving your course information, you may still update it as necessary. The form will behave precisely as before, except you are able to remove previously approved assessment methods from each CLO if need be.

Finalizing a Course

When your course information is ready to be finalized, all of the assessment fields will be made available. Fill in every field: method, mean, median, high score, satisfactory score and all of the comment fields. Once certain you are done, click finalize. Finalizing your course information will prevent you from further editing any information.

Lose the Email?

If you are unable to find the email containing the links to your courses, you can have it resent using the form found here.
